Juicebox Kids

Are you ready?

We are the new generation of creatives - youth creating art and media that inspire. Juicebox Kids helps you get the platform that your art deserves.

Youth Empowerment

Our mission at Juicebox Kids is to inspire and acknowledge the works of creatives of color throughout the world. Whether it be technology, digital design, poetry or music, we publish your work!

Digital Media

We love your digital art submissions, and are continuously posting them on our website and social media outlets.

Traditional Art

Painters, comic book artists, and anyone with a pen - we feature your work in our weekly newsletters.

Printed Issues

New season, new magazine issue full of youth creatives! Keep an eye out for our Summer 2020 issue!

Are you a creative?

Juicebox Kids was created for you, and with you. We love seeing new talent, and continue hiring on an ongoing basis. If you would like to be part of the Juicebox team, send as an email!

Not interested in committent, but still wan't to publish your work? No worries. We accept art submissions every season and share it on our Instagram, website, and even printed magazine issues. Contact us for more info, or fill out our submission form.

Our Philosophy

Empowering youth through art is our number one goal. We especially create our platform for creatives of color and LGBTQIA+ peoples. Juicebox Kids is a non-profit organizatiom, with proceeds going to Black Lives Matter funds worldwide.

We stand on trust, love, and respect. We are all here to evolve and thrive individually and with each other.
